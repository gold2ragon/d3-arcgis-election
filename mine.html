Highest Percent

This option shows the percent of the candidate who recieved the most votes in the precinct. Darker precincts correspond to a candidate who won the precinct with a larger percent of the vote, and lighter precincts correspond to a candidate who won the precinct with a lower percent of the vote.

Margin of Victory

This option shows the number of votes the candidate with the most votes won the district by. Darker precincts correspond to a candidate who won the precinct by a larger number of votes, and lighter precincts correspond to a candidate who won the precinct by a smaller number of votes.

Voter Turnout

This option shows the voter turnout for race displayed on the map. Darker precincts correspond to a higher percent of registered voters voting in the race, and lighter precincts correspond to a lower percent of registered voters voting in the race.
